Hello Everyone!!!

I am Srivarshini, I am a realistic Portrait Artist. In my childhood, I started to draw like every other child. I started from the scratch where I just scribbled whatever I saw, But never knew that it is going to be my passion. I am grateful that I've been blessed with this skill, but worked really hard to enhance and develop this gifted skill.

Always Participated in every school art competitions, and got many Prizes which boosted and motivated me to continue doing what I love. But due to certain reasons I gave up on drawing for few years. 2020 was the bestest year of my Drawing journey, I would say. Even though Covid-19 was ruling the world, the lockdown gave me time and patience to restart my Drawing journey. I practiced each and everyday to become more and more realistic portrait artist. I am thankful to my elder sister for being my teacher and motivator, who has equal responsibility to be whom I am today.

The upcoming drawings are few of my favourites that I've done (after 2020).
